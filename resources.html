List of Resources
  • Algorithm Tips – database of AI systems. investigative journalism resource exploring automated systems in the USA - http://algorithmtips.org/
  • Algorithmic ecology toolkit – Helpful infographics explaining why thinking contextually about tech deployment matters. https://freerads.org/2020/03/02/the-algorithmic-ecology-an-abolitionist-tool-for-organizing-against-algorithms/
  • Atlas of Automation – a Report broken down into themes and methodology for explaining ADMS harms. Also has a indexed database of AI systems. Very helpful and relevant project. https://atlas.algorithmwatch.org/en/
  • Data detox kit - https://datadetoxkit.org/ - Helpful, and simple resource for taking basic measures for privacy. Hope to translate civic action against ADMS in similar terms (file an RTI/ ask for your data/ what to ask of police or administrative systems, etc.)
  • AI Myths - https://www.aimyths.org/ - 
  • AI Blindspots - I like the flip cards format - Could be useful also for publication? https://aiblindspot.media.mit.edu/
  • Automating NYC - City view of automated systems - https://automating.nyc/
  • Atlas of Surveillance https://atlasofsurveillance.org/
  • Project Panoptic https://internetfreedom.in/iffs-project-panoptic-is-almost-here/
